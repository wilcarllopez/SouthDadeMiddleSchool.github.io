The Medical and Health Science Academy is a magnet program utilizing innovative strategies to engage students in investigation and discovery. This program provides rigorous content aligned with challenging academic standards in Science. Students learn the complexities of medical and health science and the interactions of the body systems through stimulating hands on laboratory and field experiences. Upon completion of the Medical and Health Sciences Magnet Program, students may choose to continue their academic studies and career preparation at various high school magnet programs offered by Miami Dade County Public Schools.
