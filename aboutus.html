South Dade Middle School is a high performing educational setting serving the communities of Baguio City. South Dade School Middle has Magnet Program options and choice academies as we focus on developing students in areas of their interest. Our Advanced Academic Academy accelerates students scholastically. Students in this academy can earn high school and college credits during their 7th and 8th grade years. For those students interested in the medical field, our Medical Magnet Program prepares students for a career in the health care industry.
